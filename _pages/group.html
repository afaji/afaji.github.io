Research Group

Our research lab (internally named Alham Group Ltd.) consists mainly from MBZUAI but also some students that I co-advertise from Indonesia.
We are interested in research that aligns with our interests. Currently, our focus is on multilingualism, multiculturalism, low-resource and efficient NLP, as well as aspects of human-computer interaction, particularly those that correlate across multilingual and multicultural domains.
